Painting is one of my many hobbies.

I am not a great illustrator. I simply enjoy the time when I paint :).

I love watercolor painting. I love dessert. I love colorful things. So I combined them all in these paintings. I have not had the chance in college to take painting class, so I simply watch tutorial on Youtube or Instagram while I paint. Some of my paint is from Kendyll Hillegas, and the objects (cupcakes) can be found on The Scran Line's Instagram.

I also especially love strawberry. They are simply so delicate and make the dessert so adorable and delicious. Everytime I walk by a pastry shop, I would be so tempted to buy a strawberry short cake.



This is a lemon mint cupcake. It looks so great and refreshing. I would totally buy one if I can, too bad they don't have this in Seattle area.
