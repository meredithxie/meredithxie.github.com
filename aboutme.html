HELLO! NICE TO MEET YOU!

I am Meredith. I am so happy you are here to see my design projects. I am always happy and open to talk!


I am an inter-discipline product designer.

Design is an inter-discipline field of study and professional career for me. I play the roles as a designer, researcher, and engineer.

Human Centered Design and Engineering is the best major I can ever found on this planet. I have found my passion of solving problems and crafting beautiful and easy-to-use products, while, in the mean time, have empathy to everything and everyone around me.

I will be graduating from University of Washington either August 2017 or December 2017, depending on how my classes are scheduled.


I love making new friends. Let's talk!
